Top Attractions


Doddabetta

Flirting with the clouds above the Nilgiris is Doddabetta, the highest peak in Tamil Nadu. Rising above the misty green valleys of Ooty standing tall at 2,637 metres, Doddabetta lives up to its name, which translates to “big mountain.”

Pine Forest

A journey of just 15 minutes from Ooty town centre will take you to the famed Pine Tree Forests of Ooty. These ancient woods are home to a wide variety of fauna, ranging from fluttering butterflies that delight the air to wild goats that roam the woods.

CSI Church of Ascension Lovedale

The Church of The Ascension is a remarkable heritage building that displays breathtaking gothic architecture.

Avalanche Lake

Avalanche Lake, a natural, crystal blue freshwater body. The lake has some of the best views of the Nilgiris, overlooking the hills draped in perfectly aligned tea shrubs and the opulent greenery of the woods.

Kodanad View Point

Kodanad view point is about 18 km from kotagiri. It is well connected and by passing through tea gardens, one can reach the view point. The ridge walk is the most adventurous part. Kodanad view point offers its visitors the breathtaking beauty of the Blue Hill Mountains and views of the plains.

Toda Village

This small pastoral community living in the Nilgiri plateau offers an excellent insight into a world quite unlike our own. The Toda people reside in small hamlets called Mund, they are rectangular in design, with a barrel-vaulted roof that reaches down to the ground. You can visit and know about their history .

Deer Park

This is one of the high-altitude animal parks in India and is easily accessible by road. The Deer Park in Ooty is a haven for Nature lovers as it is wrapped in a chilly climate and surrounded by enticing flora and fauna. The Deer Park is also home to a wide variety of birds and rabbits.

Botanical Gardens

The Government Botanical Garden in Ooty, spread over 55 acres, is a mesmerizing tourist site that has been attracting visitors for years. The Garden has a lot to offer for Nature lovers with its 600 varieties of plant species cultivated and exhibited here. The garden has a terraced layout.
